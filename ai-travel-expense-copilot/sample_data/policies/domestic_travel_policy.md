# Domestic Travel and Expense Policy

Domestic hotel reimbursement for L4 employees is allowed up to INR 5000 per night in metro cities such as Mumbai, Delhi, Bengaluru, Hyderabad, Chennai, and Pune.

Domestic hotel reimbursement for L5 employees is allowed up to INR 7000 per night in metro cities. L3 employees are allowed up to INR 3500 per night.

Cab reimbursement for local business travel is allowed up to INR 2500 per trip when supported by a valid receipt.

Meal reimbursement is allowed up to INR 1500 per day during approved business travel.

Claims with missing receipt evidence, foreign currency, policy mismatch, or unusually high amount should be routed to a human reviewer.
